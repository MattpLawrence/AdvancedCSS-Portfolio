About Me

My Name is Matthew Lawrence, people call me "Matt." I was born and raised in Atlanta Georgia, and now live in Marietta. I love doing just about anything outdoors. Hiking and mountain biking are two of my favorite activities. Some of my favorite hobbies are woodworking, cooking, and keeping Bonsai trees.

I am more classically trained as a craftsman, and mechanical engineer. While working as a Mechanical Engineer Co-Op I was given a project that involved programming databases. As I self-taught myself how to write VBA programs I discovered that I loved software development work. After working for a couple years in the Engineering field I decided that I wanted to turn programming into my main vocation. To that end, I am currently taking a full stack coding boot camp with Georgia Tech, and I am eager to learn everything I need to join the workforce as a software developer!
